REFERRING DOCTOR’S DETAILS

Please be advised if you do not bring a valid GP referral and your workers compensation claim is declined, (or your employer does not settle your accounts) you will be unable to claim medical expenses from Medicare. Please ask at reception if you require a GP referral to be done by one of our GPs at Redimed.
Please note: Some of our doctors at Redimed are not Medicare providers, therefore their accounts are not claimable from Medicare, please ask at reception for further details.

BILLING INFORMATION

If this occurs you may not be able to claim from Medicare.
