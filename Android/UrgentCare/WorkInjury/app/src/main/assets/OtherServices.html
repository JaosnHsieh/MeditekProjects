Pre-Employment Medical Assessments

REDIMED's pre-employment medicals are performed by our team of General Practitioners and Exercise Physiologists who specialise in injury screening, prevention and rehabilitation.

A Pre-Employment by REDIMED provides the employer with confirmation that their candidates have the required physical capacity to perform their job safely.

Telehealth

Our cutting edge technology enables medical consultations to take place via an IPAD from one of our REDIMED doctors to an injured worker anywhere within Australia and is available 24/7.

The benefits of Telehealth to your business are:
  • Immediate medical assessment and treatment from qualified medical practitioners.

  • Receive medical certificates and referrals online and via email following the online consult.

  • Reduce lost time injuries with workers being certified fit for work without having to leave the workplace.

Fitness for Work Assessments

An independent assessment completed by a REDIMED medical practitioner at the request of the Employer to determine an employee's fitness to return to their pre-injury role following an injury or incident.

If a physical functional assessment is required to determine the employee's physical capacity, a REDIMED Exercise Physiologist can tailor a suitable functional to assess this.
