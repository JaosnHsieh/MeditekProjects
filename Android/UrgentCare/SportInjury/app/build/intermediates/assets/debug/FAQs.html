What are your opening hours?

REDIMED clinics are open from 8.00am until 5.00pm Monday to Friday.

Do I need to provide a GP referral?

A GP referral is required if you need to see one of our specialists.

For GP, physiotherapy or hand therapy, a referral is not required.

What fees are applicable to my review?

REDIMED is a private clinic and as such fees will apply for your consultations, treatments and surgeries. For further information regarding fees payable please ring 9230 0900.
An appointment with a GP will be bulk billed through Medicare for all valid Medicare Card holders.

Can I claim any money back through my private health insurance or Medicare?

Medicare rebates apply to GP and Specialist consults for valid Medicare Card holders
For surgical rebates please contact your private health insurance or REDIMED on 9230 0900 for further information.
Physiotherapy and Hand therapy sessions rebates apply depending on your level of cover of private health insurance. For further information please call 9230 0900 or your private health insurance company.

What if I don’t have Private Health Insurance?

REDIMED will accept public, private, workers’ compensation and DVA patients. Costs associated with your consultations and treatments will be applicable. For further information please call 9230 0900.

What is the wait times to see a Specialist?

REDIMED will ensure that in most cases you will have an appointment to see a Specialist within one week. Please call 9230 0900 for the next available appointment.
